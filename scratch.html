What is Snowboarding?

Snowboarding has exploded into a global sensation over the last two decades, and everyone seems to capture it a little differently. But on this page, we’re defining it in the simplest, and truest way: the sport with the most aura.

Snowboarding is the sport with the most aura. Nobody can truly understand how fun snowboarding is until they try it. From beginners simply riding down the mountain to professionals hucking backlips off clifs, snowboading allows each individual to express themselves through the thrill of the mountain.

Here are 3 reasons why I personally enjoy snowbaording

  1. The Mountains
    • Fresh snow
    • Scenic views
    • Quiet mornings
  2. The Challenge
    • Steep runs
    • Learning tricks
    • Always something new to try
  3. The Community
    • Friends
    • Meeting new people
    • Opportunities to bond over a hobby
